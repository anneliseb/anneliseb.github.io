An introduction

Hi, I'm Annelise, a product designer with over 8 years of experience.

I am a problem solver at heart and relish the challenge of learning a new product. Problem solving comes in two parts for me. The first is understanding the problem, asking questions, and figuring out how to answer those questions. The second problem is how do we synthesize those learnings into an idea, a product and a design.

I focus on building strong design teams, establishing key partnerships, collaborating with product, research, and data to ensure we’re building the right things, and crafting smart, delightful experiences.

When I’m not designing, I can be found at the rink figure skating, in the studio dancing, and at home getting caught up in a good book that may or may not be about vampires, fairies, werewolves, or assassins.
