An introduction

I currently am shaping the future of subscription pet food at Ollie. Previously to working at Ollie, I spent just shy of 6 years at Priceline adding fare brands to the flights booking flow and redesigning Priceline’s post-booking experience.

I am a problem solver at heart. Rather than stick to a set process, I adapt to the current siutation and pull from my ever growing toolkit. I believe in setting clear goals and then having an open sandbox to achieve those goals. No designer can do it alone and nothing makes me happier at work than getting to work with a group of smart, fun individuals. Faciliting interactive, collaboration sessions is one of my specialities.

A personal goal of mine has been to grow my management and mentoring skillset. For 2 years I’ve been in a player/coach role where I have my own set of IC responsibilities, while coaching a subset of designers. Through listening and understanding the needs of each designer, I give them the guidance they require while leaving them room to navigate their own path forward.

When I’m not designing, I can be found at the ice rink figure skating, in the studio dancing, or at home getting caught up in a good book that may or may not be about vampires, fairies, werewolves, or assassins. I love to travel and prior to the 2020 was trying to do a new international trip every year.
