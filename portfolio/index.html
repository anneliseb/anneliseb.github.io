A Little Bit About Myself

Hi, I'm Annelise, a UX/UI designer with 7 years of experience.

I am a problem solver at heart and relish the challenge of learning a new product. Problem solving comes in two parts for me. The first is understanding the problem, asking questions, and figuring out how to answer those questions. The second problem is how do we synthesize those learnings into a product and design.

Most recently at Priceline.com, I led the redesign of our flights product. The flights product was Priceline.com's original product, but had not received a lot of attention over the years. We first focused on the mobile web experience, and then utilized the great work we had on mobile to create a responsive experience.

I excel when I am given ownership and a certain amount of autonomy. I love being a part of team, but I also like to work independently.

In my spare time, I can be found at the rink ice skating, in the studio dancing, or out watching a performance.
