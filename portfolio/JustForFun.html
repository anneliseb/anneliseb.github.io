A place for projects that are of a more personal nature, fall into a print or graphic design bucket, or I thoroughly enjoyed back in school.

{01} Star Wars Trading Cards

While at Pratt Institute I created a set of Star Wars trading cards for an information design class. The challenge was finding the best layout of the information that could be templated and work for each Star Wars planet and the different quantity of information for each card.

{02} Invitations

Wedding invitations created for my sister and brother-in-law. My sister and brother-in-law first met when they were children, so they requested the invitations have a playful childhood theme.

Baby shower inviations for the birth of my nephew. We decided on an animal theme for the party!

{03} Palette Knife Illustration

For a visual communocations design class, I was aksed to create an illustration of a current event. It wasn't long after U.S. Airways flight 1549 landed in the Hudson River after hitting a bird. I chose to take a comical spin on the event and show Canada Geese plsnning to take down the plane.
